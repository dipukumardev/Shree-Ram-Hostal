Frequently Asked Questions

What are the check-in and check-out times?

Check-in time is 12:00 PM and check-out time is 11:00 AM. Early check-in or late check-out can be arranged based on availability.

Do you provide meals?

Yes, we provide breakfast, lunch, and dinner. We also accommodate special dietary requirements with advance notice.

What amenities are included?

Our amenities include Wi-Fi, laundry service, study rooms, recreation areas, and 24/7 security. Some branches may have additional facilities like gyms or libraries.

What is the booking process?

You can book a room by filling out the contact form, calling us, or visiting any of our branches in person. A security deposit is required to confirm your booking.
