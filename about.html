About Us

Learn more about Shree RamBoys Hostal and our journey

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Shree RamBoys Hostal started with a simple mission: to provide comfortable, safe, and affordable accommodation for students and working professionals away from home.

What began as a single building with just 20 rooms has now expanded to multiple branches across the city, serving hundreds of residents who consider Shree RamBoys their second home.

Our founder, Mr. Ram Kumar, envisioned creating not just a place to stay, but a community where individuals could thrive, build connections, and focus on their goals without worrying about accommodation issues.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, affordable accommodation with excellent amenities and a supportive community environment that feels like home.

Our Vision

To be the leading hostel chain known for exceptional service, community building, and creating a positive impact in residents' lives.
