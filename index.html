Frequently Asked Questions

What are the check-in and check-out times?

Check-in time is 12:00 PM and check-out time is 11:00 AM. Early check-in or late check-out can be arranged based on availability.

Do you provide meals?

Yes, we provide three nutritious meals a day (breakfast, lunch, and dinner). We also accommodate special dietary requirements with advance notice.

Is there a security deposit?

Yes, we require a refundable security deposit equivalent to one month's rent. This is returned at the end of your stay, subject to room condition assessment.
